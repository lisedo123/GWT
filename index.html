We attended the AnitaB's Grace Hopper Conference, and had the incredible opportunity to interview over 100 women and girls in Tech from around the world. We asked them,

"What would you tell your younger self?"

These interviews capture valuable insights about the challenges, successes, and aspirations of women in tech. This information is crucial for bridging the gap between K-12, college, and the workforce, informing policies and strategies to encourage and support more women and girls in pursuing careers in technology.
